About us

With Solace Global, you can split your donations to multiple causes and categories. Solace Global's website features a donation page with three main categories: Medical, War, and Learning, each with specific causes for donors to support. Under the Medical category, donors can contribute to general cancer research, breast cancer, childhood cancer, heart disease, stroke, and hypertension initiatives. The War category focuses on supporting those affected by major conflicts, including the Palestine conflict, the Congo conflict, and the Yemen crisis. In the Learning category, donations can support a variety of educational efforts such as the Undefined Robotics team, providing sports equipment like soccer kits and basketball gear, and funding coding classes and programming workshops. This structure allows donors to choose causes that resonate with them and make a meaningful impact.
